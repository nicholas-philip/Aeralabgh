Frequestly Asked Questions

What is a drone light show?

A drone light show is a choreographed display of drones that are programmed to fly in a specific pattern and light up in a specific color. The drones are controlled by a computer and can be programmed to fly in a variety of shapes and patterns.

How much does a drone light show cost?

The cost of a drone light show depends on the size of the show, the number of drones, and the complexity of the show.

How long does a drone light show last?

Our drone light shows last between 5 and 10 minutes. The length of the show depends on the size of the show, the number of drones, and the complexity of the show.

How do I book a drone light show?

Booking a drone light show is easy. Simply fill out the form on our contact page and we will get back to you within 24 hours.
